Welcome, Fox Valley Girls Coding Club!

This week completes our first month as Fox Valley's only makerspace. Aside from adding 4 new members in a 2 week period, we were also lucky enough to host the Fox Valley Girl's Coding Club. The club empowers girls to learn IT skills and solve the industry's long standing diversity problem.

We're very excited to be a part of this exciting endeavor. Welcome to the community, girls!

What's Next for Fox.Build

In the coming weeks we will be continuing facility improvements, such as adding more group work stations and hosting our usual Monday free co-working day. Come stop by any Monday and check out the facility.

For those who can't make it out during business hours, we also host a First Friday Tech Social starting at 3:30pm on the first Friday of the month.

Fox.build is a great place to work, especially if you typically work from home and feel like you're missing the sense of community you might find at a traditional office. We're a friendly bunch and are always looking for new members.
