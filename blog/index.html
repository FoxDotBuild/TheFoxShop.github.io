Computer Vision, Beer Friday, New Members

Feb 7 2016. By: Rick

Happy weekend, Fox Valley!

Yesterday was our monthly "First Friday Happy Hour". We had a good turn out and even grew our membership. Welcome to the community, Danny and Paul!

Aside from continuing the X-Carve build, we were also given a sneak peek at [new member] Paul's CoinSorter device.

Coin sorter leverages computer vision and machine learning to quickly identify and sort small parts. The use case Paul is tackling currently is quickly sorting through coins to find currency from specific years.

If you weren't able to meet us this time around, why not check out the events page and RSVP for our next event? We look forward to meeting you!
